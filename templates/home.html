Welcome to the Big Mart Sales Prediction

Project Overview

This project aims to predict sales for Big Mart products based on various factors. By analyzing historical data and input features, we strive to provide accurate sales forecasts to help improve inventory management and decision-making.

How It Works

Users can input specific item details, and our model will generate a prediction for the sales quantity of the product. This can help businesses better plan their inventory and sales strategies.
